Paul's Testimony to King Agrippa

Having therefore obtained help of God, I continue unto this day, witnessing both to small and great, saying none other things than those which the prophets and Moses did say should come: That Christ should suffer, and that he should be the first that should rise from the dead, and should shew light unto the people, and to the Gentiles.

My Own Testimony

I know that Christ truly does live. I have seen his hand in my life many times. He has comforted me when I needed comfort, he strengthened me when I was weak, and he loved me when I felt no love. I know that Christ deeply loves every living soul on this planet. I know that he went through unimaginable pain, so we can have the opportunity to see him in heaven. Jesus Christ is our savior and our redeemer. He has done the impossible for you and me. I hope that I live a righteous life, so I may thank him in person.
